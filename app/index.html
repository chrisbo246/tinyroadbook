Minimalist route planner

Retrieve city names with few clicks on a map, freely edit your minimalist roadbook, print it... You are ready for the great escape!

Advantages of a minimalist paper roadbook

Convenient

done
  • You have an overview of your route always on hand.
  • You can quickly ask your route without speaking local language.
  • It does not take up space and weighs nothing.
  • It fits in your back pocket and is perfectly suitable for your butt.

Anti stress

tag_faces
  • You can travel for months without recharging the battery.
  • It prevent you to sit and cry when your smartphone / GPS will fail.
  • You can show your route without fear of being robbed like an idiot.
  • You can have many paper and numeric backups.

Cheap

money_off
  • It cost the price of a sheet of paper.
  • You do not need to buy a new (paper or GPS) map every two weeks.
  • You do not need any expensive solar panel, converter, dynamo hub, etc...
  • It can save you a nice surprise (bill) if you forget to turn off data.

Features

edit

Simple text editor

Edit your roadbook as you see fit by structuring your route or adding comments via the integrated text editor.

add_location

City name extractor

No need to manually write your roadbook. You can retrieve city names by clicking your pois on the map.

timeline

GPX viewer

Download a GPS track from a sharing website and select it in settings. Your road will show up on the map.

directions_bike

Cycle route planner

Activate the Opencyclemap bikemap or Lonvia cycling layer and follow an official cycle route to plan your epic bike tour.
