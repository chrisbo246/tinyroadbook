Minimalist road book editor

Extract city names with just few click on a map, freely edit your minimalist road book and live your adventure!

Why a minimalist and old-fashioned paper road book?

  • You have an overall view of your route and never lose your goal.
  • You can travel for months without recharging the battery.
  • You can request your road, even with your incomprehensible pronunciation.
  • You are not afraid of being robbed like an idiot when asking your route.
  • It fits in your back pocket and is perfectly suitable for your butt.
  • You do not need to buy a new map every two weeks.
  • You can have a backup in your backpack for few cents.
  • That is all you need if you are looking for adventure.

Features

directions_bike

Cycle route planner

You are planning a bike tour? This application is provided with the Opencyclemap bikemap and the Lonvia cycling layer. Just follow one of the official bike routes to plan your epic bike trip.

short_text

Text editor

Why add complexity when you need flexibility? Customize your road book as you see fit by structuring your route, highlighting the cities to visit or adding comments via the integrated text editor.

timeline

GPX viewer

You'd like to preview your route to simplify your roadbook edition? Just download a GPX file from a GPS track sharing website and select it from settings. Your road will show up on the map.
